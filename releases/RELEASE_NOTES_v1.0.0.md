### Installation Instructions

#### Installing the APK
1. Download the `split-expenses-v1.0.0.apk` file
2. On your Android device, go to **Settings** → **Security**
3. Enable **Install from Unknown Sources** (or **Install Unknown Apps** on newer Android versions)
4. Open the downloaded APK file
5. Tap **Install**
6. Once installed, tap **Open** to launch the app

> **Note**: You may see a warning about installing apps from unknown sources. This is normal for apps not downloaded from the Play Store.

## 📖 How to Use

### Getting Started
1. **Launch the app** and create an account or skip login for local use
2. **Create a group** by tapping the + button
3. **Add participants** from contacts or manually
4. **Add expenses** and select who paid and who to split with
5. **View balances** in the Summary tab

### Key Screens
- **Group List**: View all your expense groups
- **Group Details**: See expenses and total spending
- **Add Expense**: Record new shared expenses
- **Expense Details**: View breakdown of each expense
- **Summary**: See who owes whom and settle up
